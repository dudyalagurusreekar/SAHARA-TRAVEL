Temple Overview

History

The temple of Lord Venkateswara is located on the seventh peak of Tirumala Hills. According to ancient scriptures, the temple has been in existence for thousands of years and is considered one of the most sacred places in Hinduism.

Architecture

The temple is built in Dravidian architecture with the main tower (Vimana) covered in pure gold. The temple complex spans several acres and includes various mandapams and sub-shrines.

Significance

Lord Venkateswara is believed to be the incarnation of Vishnu who appeared here to save mankind from the troubles of Kali Yuga. The temple is one of the richest and most visited religious places in the world.
